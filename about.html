ABOUT US
OUR STORY:

We are a group of students who are trying to provide a better environment for students like us to find info about courses more easily! We are dedicated to provide a platform to allow everyone to share their documents and reviews on all courses offered in McGill University, in hope that such information will be useful for future students.



OUR TEAM:
